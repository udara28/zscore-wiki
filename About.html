The nature has given a gift to every living creature. I believe human ability to learn is our gift. It is truely remakable what we have accomplished as a species by the knowledge we gathered by learning. Thanks to my teachers, I developed a true passion in science. I created zscore.lk with the vission to transform the learning experience of A/L science students. I wanted to help students discover the beauty of science as my teachers had done for me.
