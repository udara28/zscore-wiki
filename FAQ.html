FAQ

What is zscore.lk

zscore.lk is the first science app which can help you prep for A/L exam. It allows you to attempt quizes, watch lectures and tutorials conduct by quality lecturers.

How to try a quiz in zscore.lk

To attempt a quiz in zscore you need to sign in first. You can easily sign in to zscore.lk using your google account. Then you can open a quiz from here

Can I use zscore.lk without a google account

You can create a legacy account using your email address. However we recommend logging with google because it gives better security and next time you visit zscore.lk, google users will be automatically logged you in.
